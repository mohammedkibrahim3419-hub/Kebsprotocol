How it works
Three steps. Zero friction.
1
Set the condition. Define what triggers a payment — engagements, delivery, time, or any on-chain event.
2
Fund the pool. Deposit USDC into the smart contract. It sits there until the condition is met.
3
Contract executes. Condition met → USDC moves instantly. On-chain proof of every transaction.
